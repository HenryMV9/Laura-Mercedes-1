Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help you with all your queries

Payment Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), bank transfers, and PayPal. We also provide secure online payment options through Stripe.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to protect your financial information. All transactions are processed securely.

Shipping Questions

How long does shipping take?

+

Within Abuja: 1-3 business days. Nigeria-wide: 3-7 business days. International shipping: 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we offer international shipping to select countries. Shipping rates and delivery times vary based on the destination. Contact us for specific international shipping details.

Hair Care Questions

How do I care for my extensions or wig?

+

We provide detailed care instructions with each product. Generally, use sulfate-free shampoo, cool water, and avoid excessive heat. For personalized advice, book a beauty consultation.

Are your hair products human hair?

+

Yes, we exclusively use premium quality human hair for our wigs, extensions, and hair products. Each piece is carefully sourced and processed to ensure the highest quality.

Installation Questions

Do you offer professional wig installation?

+

Absolutely! We provide professional wig installation services in our Abuja salon. Our expert stylists ensure a perfect fit, natural look, and personalized styling.

How long does a wig installation take?

+

A typical wig installation takes 2-3 hours, depending on the complexity of the style and the specific techniques used. We recommend booking a consultation for a precise timeline.
